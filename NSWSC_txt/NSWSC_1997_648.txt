

Ryan versus Nixons Blayney Pty Ltd; Ryan versus Nixons Transport Pty Ltd Matter Nos /97 [1997] NSWSC 648 (15 December 1997) 
Denis Ryan versus Nixons Blayney Pty Ltd /97 December 1997 
Howie AJ 
The Supreme Court of New South Wales Common Law Division /97 DENIS RYAN V NIXONS BLAYNEY PTY LTD 
JUDGMENT 
For the reasons I gave in relation to the case of Sharon Holland versus Nixons
Transport Pty Ltd (12953/97) delivered today, I hold that the determination of the magistrate in dismissing the information was erroneous on the point of law and I remit the matter to the magistrate to be determined according to law. I order the defendant to pay the plaintiff's costs.
