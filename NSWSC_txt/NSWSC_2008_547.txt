JUDGMENT In each of these proceedings, the plaintiff applies under [s
G](http://www.austlii.edu.au/au/legis/cth/consolact/ca2001172/s459g.html) of the [Corporations Act ](http://www.austlii.edu.au/au/legis/cth/consolact/ca2001172/) (Cth) for an order setting aside a statutory demand served on it by the defendant.
 It was accepted by the parties in each case that the decision in Clawson
Holdings Pty Ltd versus Citigroup Global Markets Australia Pty Ltd <case> would govern.
 In each of 1521/08 and 1522/08, therefore, it is ordered that the proceedings be dismissed with costs.
