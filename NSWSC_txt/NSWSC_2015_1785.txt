

Rothnere Pty Limited versus Magic Markets Pty Limited [2015] NSWSC 1785 (24
November 2015) 
Last Updated: 30 November 2015 
Supreme Court 
New South Wales  
Decision: 
JUDGMENT  
The defendants jointly and severally are to pay the plaintiff the sum of $837,856.06 such sum being comprised of the principal amount of $802,910 and interest in the amount of $34,946.06.  
THE COURT ORDERS THAT:  
The cross claim filed 22 February 2014 is dismissed.  
The defendants are to pay the plaintiff's costs on an ordinary basis up to and including 5 February 2015 and on an indemnity basis from 5 February 2015.  
The defendants are to pay the plaintiff interest on costs pursuant to [s (4)](http://www.austlii.edu.au/au/legis/nsw/consolact/cpa2005167/s101.html) of the [Civil Procedure Act ](http://www.austlii.edu.au/au/legis/nsw/consolact/cpa2005167/) .  
Catchwords: 
DEBT RECOVERY - lessor seeks recovery from lessee for amounts payable under registered lease in respect of retail premises - defendants absent at hearing - procedure under rule 29.7 [Uniform Civil Procedure Rules ](http://www.austlii.edu.au/au/legis/nsw/consolreg/ucpr2005305/) -
